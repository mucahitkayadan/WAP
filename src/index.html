About Chickens

Chickens are domesticated birds that are primarily raised for their meat and eggs. They are a common farm animal and are found in many households around the world.

Types of Chickens

  • Broilers: Raised for meat production.
  • Layers: Raised for egg production.
  • Bantams: Small-sized chickens often kept as pets.

Characteristics of Chickens

  • Feathers: Chickens have feathers that come in various colors and patterns.
  • Beak: They have a beak that they use for pecking and eating.
  • Claws: Chickens have claws on their feet for scratching the ground.

Interesting Facts

  • Chickens are descendants of the red junglefowl.
  • They have excellent color vision.
  • Chickens can fly short distances, but they prefer to stay on the ground.
